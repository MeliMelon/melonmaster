With some people this can be considered torture, crazyness, insanity. So for some people, living vicariously is out of the picture.

Cookies always taste amazing. Key word there, TASTE. So taste is what makes cookies taste good. But what MAKES cookies taste good? Sugar. Why does sugar taste good? Sugar tastes good because it releases a certain chemical in our brains called dopamine. Dopamine is what makes things tastes good, makes things feel good, it is the happy chemical. It directly corresponds with why you over indulge as well. See, because when you eat the first cookie your brain releases a lot of dopamine, and you feel happy. Then when you eat that second cookie, your brain releases more dopamine, third cookie more dopamine. You like this because obviously it means that you are happy. So to keep this dopamine up, you eat the entire box of cookies plus 32 more. You feel sick, horrible, and stupid for doing that, but you are happy. Extremely happy. So the problem here is the dopamine. The dopamine makes us happy, so maybe if we didn't have that dopamine we could control ourselves. But if we didn't have that dopamine, the cookies would taste more like salad, which is gross compared to cookies. So what do we do? What if we conditioned our brain to release LESS dopamine when eating cookies. It is definitley possible.
